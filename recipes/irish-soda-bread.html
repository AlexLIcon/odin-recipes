Irish Soda Bread

Description

Possibly unfamiliar to those who are not Irish or Irish American, Soda Bread is sweet, delicious treat. It can be enjoyed any day of the year, but I prefer to have it on St. Patrick's Day!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 4 ounces of butter, softened
  • 1 cup of granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1.5 cups of buttermilk
  • 1 tablespoon of caraway seeds
  • 1 cup of raisins

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine all ingredients and mix until well blended. Note, the batter will be stiff.
  2. Butter a 10inch frying pan.
  3. Place the batter in the pan and slash an X in the top with a knife. Please be careful.
  4. Bake for 1 hour at 350 Degrees Farenheit.
  5. Serve toasted or untoasted with butter or jam.
