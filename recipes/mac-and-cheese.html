mac-and-cheese

Description

You know it. You love it no childhood is complete without trying a bowl of Mac and Cheese. Wheather you add Bacon, Chicken or just eat it regular, its hard to forget a great bowl of Mac and Cheese.

Just follow these easy steps and you can have great Mac and Cheese in your home in a matter of minutes!

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Cheese
  • Milk
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Bring a pot to a boil
  2. Place in the Macaroni
  3. Once the Macaroni is soft, strain the water.
  4. Add the Butter, Milk, and Cheese
  5. Stir until the ready
